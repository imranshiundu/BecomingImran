About

Imran Shiundu is a builder working across software, product systems, AI tools, trading infrastructure, legal-tech, creative archives, and practical web experiences.

The site is designed as a cinematic archive: one page, heavy typography, restrained motion, and enough space for the work to feel deliberate without pretending the story is finished.

The direction is simple: build useful systems, document the receipts, keep the presentation sharp, and let the archive mature as the work becomes stronger.
